Keep your AI agent
on a leash

SafeClaw is the safety layer that watches every action your AI agent takes — and lets you approve or block anything dangerous. Install in 3 steps, no experience needed.

Get started

3 steps. That's it.

1

Download Node.js

Node.js is a free tool that runs SafeClaw. Download it, open the installer, and click "Next" until it's done.

Download Node.js (free)

Already have Node.js? Skip to step 2.

2

Open Terminal

Terminal is an app already on your computer. Here's how to find it:

Press Cmd + Space, type Terminal, hit Enter
Press Win + R, type cmd, hit Enter
Press Ctrl + Alt + T to open Terminal
3

Paste this command and hit Enter

Copy the command below, paste it into Terminal, and press Enter. SafeClaw opens in your browser automatically.

$ npx safeclaw

What happens next: Your browser opens a setup wizard. It walks you through choosing your AI provider and entering your API key. Everything stays on your computer — nothing is sent to us.

What does SafeClaw do?

Blocks dangerous actions

Your AI agent can't delete files, run risky code, or access sensitive data without your permission.

You approve everything

When your agent wants to do something risky, SafeClaw pauses and asks you first. You decide yes or no.

Full audit trail

Every action your agent takes is logged with tamper-proof records. See exactly what happened and when.

Works with Claude & OpenAI

Use your preferred AI provider. SafeClaw wraps around any agent and adds the safety layer on top.
